Description

Creamy white sauce pasta with veggies is one of my favorite pasta recipes. It‚Äôs cheesy and oh so good! Packed with veggies, this is a great way to make kids eat their veggies! I like to serve it with a side of garlic bread for that perfect comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta: I like using penne pasta probably because that‚Äôs how I always had it in India but you can definitely use other pasta shapes. Macaroni and Rigatoni will also work well. I personally don‚Äôt like using spaghetti.
  • Veggies:You can use any veggies that you like in this pasta, the day I made this pasta, I had peppers and broccoli at home and that‚Äôs what I used in the recipe along with onion and sweet corn. I also use tons of garlic because garlic just goes so well in this pasta.
  • Milk: I like using whole milk but 2% would also work. Adding cream makes it richer.
  • Seasonings: I have added dried Italian seasoning and dried oregano, you can use herbs like basil and thyme as well. Add more or less of the seasoning and adjust to taste.

Directions:

  1. Boil 150 grams of pasta according to instructions on the package. I used regular penne pasta which was supposed to be boiled for 9 to 11 minutes. and drain. Make sure you don‚Äôt overcook your pasta, you want it al-dente. Note: use only 1 cup pasta if you like more sauce in your pasta. While the pasta is boiling chop all the veggies.
  2. Once the pasta is boiled, drain it using a colander and set aside.
  3. Melt 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan on medium heat. Add all the veggies ‚Äì
    • small red onion, chopped
    • 1 medium red pepper, sliced
    • 4 cup small broccoli florets
    • 2 cup sweet corn (frozen corn which I added to hot water for 5 minutes before using).
  4. Cook the veggies for 2 minutes. They should remain crunchy. You can use other veggies like carrots, peas as well.
  5. Once done, remove veggies onto a plate.
  6. To another pan or you can use the same pan, now add 1 tablespoon olive oil and the remaining tablespoon of butter.
  7. Once the butter melts, add the 5-6 chopped garlic cloves and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  8. Then add in 1 & 1/2 tablespoons of flour.
  9. Whisk the flour continuously, using a wire whisk for around 30 to 60 seconds. You don‚Äôt have to brown the flour much.
  10. Then add in 2 cups of milk. You can also add 1/4 cup heavy cream here or use additional 1/4 cup milk.
  11. Mix everything using a whisk so that there are no lumps formed.
  12. Add in the seasonings:
    • 1 teaspoon Italian seasonings
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (to taste)
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
    • black pepper, to taste
  13. Mix in everything and let the sauce simmer.
  14. Sauce will thicken as it simmers and in 4 to 5 minutes it will coat the back of the spoon. Turn heat to lowest.
  15. Add in the veggies and the boiled pasta.
  16. Toss until the pasta and veggies are coated with the sauce. Taste test and adjust the seasonings at this point. Serve warm!
